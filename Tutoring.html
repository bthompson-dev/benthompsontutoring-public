Home Tutoring

I am happy to tutor at your home - I enjoy in-person tutoring as it is often easier to connect with students and guide them when they may be having problems. It also gives me an opportunity to feedback directly to parents and chat about the progress of their son or daughter.

However, as I am based in Clapham, I can only travel to local areas (approximately within a 3 mile radius). This is because I like to travel by bike, so can't cover huge distances!

Please see the map for an idea of where I travel to.

 

Online Tutoring

Since the pandemic, the majority of my tutoring has been online!

While there can be technical issues to deal with, there are other significant advantages.

These include:

  • Flexibility
  • The ability to save and rewatch lessons
  • Tutoring anywhere in the world

I usually use a platform called Bramble for my online sessions. It's simple and user-friendly, and I've found it really effective in encouraging students of all ages to work attentively and confidently online.

Pricing & Details

13+ / GCSE £45
A-Level / IB £50

My usual hours for tutoring are weekdays from 4 - 8.30pm.

Occasionally I do tutor earlier in the day, so please enquire if you're interested in an earlier time. Please note I do not tutor on weekends.

Payment is usually by bank transfer in advance of the lesson.

There is an additional £10 charge for Home Tutoring, to cover travel time.

I offer the first session guaranteed, so if you are unhappy for any reason, you can claim a full refund.

If you need to cancel a session that has already been booked, please get in contact as soon you can. I will always try to rearrange for a different time if possible. Otherwise, for cancellations with under 24 hours notice, a cancellation charge of 50% of the full price will apply (if the session cannot be rearranged).
